My Expenditure Tracker

This Project of ours keeps track of the money an indiviual spends.

We have basically tried to maintain record of what you spend, what you earn,

in terms of credit and debit while also keeping track of payment methods to help user get hold of what he spent and from where.

Bank links are also provided to prevent extra chaos and hustle. Basically we tried to get all the things at a common place rather than visiting a ton of websites and downloading apps.
